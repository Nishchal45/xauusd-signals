1
Go to github.com and sign in

2
Create a new repository

Upload the 4 app files
In your new repo, click "Add file" → "Upload files"

2
Deploy on Render.com
render.com · free · 5 minutes
1
Sign up at render.com
Go to render.com → click "Get Started for Free"
Sign up with your GitHub account (easiest — links automatically)
2
Create a new Web Service
In your Render dashboard:
Click "New +" → "Web Service"
3
Connect your GitHub repository
Click "Connect account" → authorize GitHub access
Search for xauusd-signals → click "Connect"
4
Configure the deployment settings
Fill in exactly as shown:
FIELDVALUE
Name xauusd-signals
Region Ohio (US East)
or closest to you

Wait for the build to finish (~3 minutes)
Render will install packages and start your server.
You'll see log output. Wait until you see:

Copy your live URL
At the top of your Render dashboard you'll see a URL like:

Copy this URL — you'll need it for UptimeRobot and your phone.
💡
Test it now: Open the URL in your browser. You should see the gold signal dashboard loading live data.
3
Set Up UptimeRobot
uptimerobot.com · free · keeps app always awake
–
⚠️
Why you need this: Render's free tier puts your app to sleep after 15 minutes of no traffic. The first load after sleep takes 30+ seconds. UptimeRobot pings your app every 5 minutes to keep it awake 24/7 — for free.
1
Create a free account
Go to uptimerobot.com → click "Register for FREE"
Sign up with your email. No credit card needed.
Verify your email address.
2
Add a new monitor
In your UptimeRobot dashboard:
Click "+ Add New Monitor"
3
Configure the monitor exactly like this
FIELDVALUE
Monitor Type HTTP(s)
Friendly Name XAU/USD Signal App
URL (or IP) https://xauusd-signals.onrender.com/health
← paste YOUR Render URL + /health at the end
Monitoring Interval 5 minutes
free plan minimum
Alert Contacts Your email
optional — alerts if app goes down
💡
The /health endpoint is a lightweight ping route built into the app — it returns instantly without fetching gold data, so it's perfect for keep-alive pings.
4
Click "Create Monitor"
UptimeRobot will immediately do a first check.
Within 1 minute you'll see a green "UP" status badge.

That's it — your app will now stay awake 24/7.
✅
Green badge = app is live and awake. UptimeRobot will email you if it ever goes down.
5
Verify the health endpoint works
Open this URL in your browser (replace with your actual URL):
https://xauusd-signals.onrender.com/health
You should see:
{"status": "ok", "time": "2024-01-15T10:30:00Z"}
If you see this, UptimeRobot will monitor it successfully.

TROUBLESHOOTING
App loads slowly (30s)
UptimeRobot not set up yet, or just started. Normal — wait for first ping.
UptimeRobot shows DOWN
Check Render logs. Usually a package install issue. Redeploy from Render dashboard.
"Not enough data" error
Yahoo Finance timeout. Refresh the page in 60 seconds.
Build failed on Render
Check the build log. Usually a package version mismatch. Re-upload requirements.txt
Wrong gold price showing
Market closed (weekend/holiday). Shows last available close — expected.
Want to change risk %
Edit main.py on GitHub, change risk_usd = 100. Render auto-redeploys in 2 min.
